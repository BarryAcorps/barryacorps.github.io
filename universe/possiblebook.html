Hi. I know I already made a post today, but I should also update you on the “Barry Acorps” universe. I already have an idea for a book series.

This book series is going to focus on a detective character that I recently made. She’s inspired by a lot of characters, mostly smart characters. I always wanted to write a book with a smart character doing their own smart things. The only problem is how I would find the target audience.

There’s probably a lot of mystery novels out there, and I don’t know how I would stand out. I haven’t heard a lot of mystery novels, so it’s either that there’s a lot of space in the mystery genre, or that a lot of mystery books just don’t get popular. Thinking that I won’t stand out won’t do anything, though I’ll just try to figure it out while I’m writing.

I been talking a lot, so I’ll just minimize it to my ideas. It’s written in a third person view (I might change it though), and it’s in the perspective of another character that is also recently made. Fun fact: If you know one of my characters already, they are the son of that character. I won’t reveal it though, but I think some of you might have an idea.

The setting is 2010, and they are 14-year-olds attending a middle school. The first case happens in said middle school, but the rest is undecided. The first book is going to be a 5-case book with around a hundred thousand words. Every case is going to be released 3 months from each other so I have enough time. The reason why I said it’s a book series is because I plan for it to have sequels, written when they are a different age. A book corresponds to how old the detective character is.

Though, this is all just ideas. None of this is final because I am constantly improving it. I will update you more on this website.

See you.
